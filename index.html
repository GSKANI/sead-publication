Package Overview



The Starter Package is meticulously tailored to support creative voices taking their first major steps into print publication. Ideal for short-fiction titles, poetry collections, philosophical essays, and motivational guides, this package includes every professional element needed to launch an exceptional product.

We provide premium customized layout design, detailed cover typesetting, and professional proofreading to ensure your manuscript meets rigorous commercial retail standards. Rather than relying on simple templates, our internal design team works with your concept to align theme with layout aesthetic.

To eliminate capital risk, we employ a sophisticated Print-on-Demand (POD) distribution network. Your book is printed directly as purchases occur, meaning zero storage inventory overhead, zero stockout risk, and seamless global fulfillment.

Frequently Asked Questions



The package covers comprehensive copyediting, custom high-resolution cover layout, interior formatting for paperback and ebook formats, custom ISBN assignment, listing on Amazon/Flipkart, and continuous royalty reports.

We use a state-of-the-art Print-on-Demand system. Whenever a customer buys a book, our distribution partner prints and ships it instantly. You do not have to pay for bulk printing upfront.

Authors retain 100% of the net profit (MRP minus print costs and channel fees). Royalties are tracked in our dashboard and transferred to your bank account monthly.

Interactive Manuscript Formatting Guide

Ensure your document matches retail printing metrics. Select parameters below to view standard specifications.

Formatting Specifications

  • Standard Margins: 0.5 inches (Gutter 0.13 inches)
  • Line Spacing: 1.15x to 1.25x
  • Recommended Fonts: Garamond, Georgia, or Palatino (11pt / 12pt)
  • First Line Indents: 0.2 inches (No tab spacing)
  • Chapter Headings: H1, centered, starts 1/3 down the page
